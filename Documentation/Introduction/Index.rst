What does it do?
----------------

The extension contains the svt-videwhelper and its classes.
The viewhelper use a svg-file like a template.
After filling the svg with your dynamic content the svt-viewhelper integrates the svg-code in your html5-template.

The shortname *svt* mean "Sexy Variable Templating".
